## Critical Digital Literacy in Action

<div class="grid">
  <article class="card">
    <h3>Platforms and Tools</h3>
    <p>No-code, low-code, and developer-oriented tools for maps, timelines, scrollytelling, audio, annotation, games, 3D, zines, and prototypes.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="card">
    <h3>Sources and Collections</h3>
    <p>Open and mixed-rights repositories for cultural heritage materials, texts, images, sound, geodata, research data, and reuse-aware story work.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="card">
    <h3>Examples for Critique</h3>
